🔧 Our Services

Everything your phone needs,
fixed right.

From dead motherboards to shattered screens — we handle all hardware repairs with precision tools and genuine parts.

🔬
Chip Level Repair

Advanced micro-soldering and IC component replacement on logic boards. We fix dead-short faults, power failures, and burned ICs at component level.

🖥️
Motherboard Repair

Full board-level diagnostics and repair for all smartphone brands. Dead phone or board short — we revive it when others give up.

📱
Screen Replacement

Original quality display replacements for Samsung, Vivo, Oppo, Realme, Redmi, iPhone and more. Crystal-clear results every time.

🔋
Battery Replacement

Fast drain or not charging at all? We replace with quality batteries to restore full battery performance and lifespan.

📷
Camera & Port Repair

Blurry or cracked camera? Faulty charging port? We handle front & rear camera replacements and USB/charging port repairs.

🔧
All Hardware Repairs

Speaker, mic, back panel, buttons — if it's a hardware problem, consider it handled. We cover all major brands, all models.

⚠️   We specialize in hardware repairs only — software issues are not handled.
